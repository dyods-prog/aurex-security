Common Questions

How does the app protect my phone?

The app provides comprehensive protection for your device by detecting and blocking malicious apps, phishing websites, and other digital threats. It helps keep your personal data safe, alerts you to suspicious activity, and prevents unauthorized access. The protection runs in the background without slowing down your phone.

Is the app safe to use?

Yes. The app is designed with security and privacy in mind. It uses industry-standard protection technologies, does not access personal data without permission, and operates safely in the background to protect your device without affecting performance.

Do I need a subscription to use the app?

Yes, a subscription is required to use the app. Subscribing gives you full access to all features and continuous protection for your device.

How can I cancel my subscription?

You can cancel your subscription at any time through your App Store account settings. Once canceled, you will continue to have access to your subscription features until the end of the current billing period.

How do I request a refund?

To request a refund, please contact the App Store support team, depending on where you purchased the subscription. Refunds are handled according to their policies, and you may be asked to provide your order details.
